User Profile and Account Management


Available on the top-right section of your dashboard or home page, is the Manage Account section. From here the user logged into the system can view or edit their profile, access the API tokens, or Logout of the billing platform.



We will have a look at the options available with each:

User Profile


The first available option presented on the list when clicking on your credentials is; Profile.

There are five editable options available to update your personal preferences or information.


The following options describes the processes related to each:

Option 1: The user can update their account's profile by adding a new photo, and changing their account user name and/or e-mail address.


Option 2: The user can change their current password with a new password. To ensure the password stays secure, it is recommended to use at least 8 digits.


Option 3: Enable two factor authentications. When this feature is enabled, the user will receive a random secure token during authentication which can be retrieved from their phone's Google Authenticator application.


Option 4: Logout of other browser sessions, allows the user to logout of all their browser sessions across all their devices. In the case that the user suspect that their account was compromised, it is advisable that they also change their current password.


Option 5: : A user can delete their account from the system if this is required. It should be noted that once an account was deleted, all the resources and data associated with the account will also be deleted.




API Tokens

API tokens allows third-party application to connect with our application on your behalf.

To navigate to the API Tokens, select the second option available on the user profile list; API Tokens

An API manual is available on request.


The user has two options to choose from when selecting API tokens:

Option 1: Select ALL API tokens to be used.



Option 2: Select only certain API tokens to be used.


Once you have your preferred selection of tokens, click on the Create option at the bottom of this API section.

Result: Once the API Token was selected, you will receive a copy of the token. For security reasons the token will not be shown again, the information should be saved.





Logout


From the Manage Account section at the bottom,the user can logout of the Numista system by clicking on the Logout option.

Result: When this option is selected, the user will be redirected to the Numista Login page.



Creating a New Customer


You can create a new customer manually to allow for billing from your Numista billing platform. The following sections describes the steps.

When logged into Numista, the dashboard will present all your active customers.

Prerequisites:

1. If you are not on the customer page, select Customers from the top menu.

2. From the customer page to the right of your screen, click on New Customer

The following is required when a new customer is created:

Step 1: Provide the Customer Name



Step 2: Provide the Company Name

This will be the company name associated with the customer or reseller, where applicable.



Step 3: : Confirm if the customer will be billable from Numista for services or products.

Step 4: Confirm if this is a Bill on Behalf customer

If you will be billing this customer on behalf of a reseller, this field is marked as: Yes

If the customer is one of your organization's direct customers, this field should be marked as: No





Step 4.1: If the Bill on Behalf customer is marked as yes, there will be an additional field: Please select your reseller

To select a reseller, type the first letter and then select the correct reseller from the list:



Step 5: Confirm the Customer Type

There are three customer types available to choose from; Reseller, Customer or Wholesaler.



Reseller, can be one of two types

Direct Billing, these resellers are billed on their cost rate, and they are responsible to charge their own customer at the end of each billing date.

Bill on Behalf Customers of business partners receives their monthly billing from you as the wholesale providers, and the business partner in turn receives a monthly rebate or profit share in accordance to their agreement.

Customers, can be one of two types

Direct Customers, these are your direct customer and receives monthly billing directly from you as the wholesale provider.

Bill on Behalf, these are customers of your 'Bill on Behalf' resellers, for whom you do monthly billing on their behalf. These customers receive monthly invoices from you, and payment is made into your bank account.

Wholesaler, is usually a company that provides products and services to their resellers. In turn their resellers provide these services and products to their customers.

Step 6: Confirm the Discount Level applicable to the client, if any.

Discount levels can be given to resellers, customer or wholesale partners, depending on their agreement.

There are four types of discount levels: 0%, 15%, 20% or 30%

These levels can be customised to suit your organizational needs.





Step 7: Provide the customers Email Adress

This will be the email address of your client who will receive all notifications from the billing platform.

Step 8: Provide the customer Mobile Number

This will be the mobile or contact number of your client.

Step 9: When all the information was captured correctly, click on: Save

Result: The new customer will be added and the process will be complete
