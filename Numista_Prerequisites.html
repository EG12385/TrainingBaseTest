Initial System Configuration

Before you can start benefitting from your Numista billing platform, there needs to be some actions taken first to make your system fully functional according to your organizations requirements.


We at ICTLabs will assist with the initial setup of your billing platform, the following tasks provides an overview of important information and actions required in order to complete your custom setup:

  • Company Preferences
    • Company Logo
    • Colour / Theme Preference
  • Financial System Details
    • Xero / Sage / Pastel
  • Banking Details
    • For clients to make payments to you for their received invoices, we need the following information:
    • Beneficiary Name
    • Bank
    • Branch Code
    • Account Number
  • Terms and Conditions
      • The T's & C's can either be added on your invoice templates, or we can provide a link to your website's terms and conditions.
  • System Integration
      • If there are any other systems that you need to be integrated with your billing platform, we will require additional information and/or access. Examples includes; VSP Portal, ClientZone, Netcash, or other systems
  • If we are not managing your domain, we will provide you with a-records to be added on yuor DNS management to access your portal.
  • We need confirmation of the unique FQDN to be used for your portal
  • For the system to send emails on your behalf, we will provide you with the server's information to be added to your domain SPF records

Getting Started


Before you can access Numista, there is some actions required first


1. This section will describe the User Registration process


Step 1: THe user needs to brows to the unique FQDN URL, as configured during the initial setup process, ending with: /register



Step 2: The person who received the registraion link needs to create their own account and password to access Numista



Step 3: Click on Register to access Numista


Result: The user will be redirected to the Numista platform





2. Adding a User Role to a new user:

Prerequisite: Once the user is registered, the administrator needs to login to their own profile to view the team members currently registered on Numista


Step 1: From the Team Members section, the user will be registered as an Affiliate. In order to change the role click on Affiliate



Step 2: Administrator can select the appropriate Role to be assigned to the new user


The following roles is available to select:

Administrator Can perform any action
Accountant Has the ability to update billing
Product Manager Can alter products
Sales Manager
Sales Manager & CA
Sales Agent
CEM
Affiliate Business Partner

Step 3: Once the role was selected, click on; Save


Result: The new user can login or refresh their page. They will have permissions to perform certain tasks from the Numista menu according to their assigned role
